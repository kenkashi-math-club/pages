問A2 解答  answer

解法

\(\displaystyle \int_\alpha^\beta f(x)dx +\int_\beta^\gamma f(x)dx = \int_\alpha^\gamma f(x)dx\)， \(\displaystyle \int_{-a}^a x^{2n}dx = 2\int_0^a x^{2n}dx,\quad \int_{-a}^a x^{2n-1} dx =0\: \text{（\(n\)は整数）}\) を利用すると， \begin{align} &\int_{-4}^{-1} (2x^3-4x-5)dx + \int_{-1}^4 (2x^3-4x-5)dx\\ =&\int_{-4}^{4} (2x^3-4x-5)dx\\ =&2\int_0^4 -5dx\\ =&2\left[ -5x \right]_0^4\\ =&\mathbf{-40} \end{align}
