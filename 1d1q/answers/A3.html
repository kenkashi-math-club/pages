問A3 解答  answer

解法

\(\displaystyle \int_{-a}^a x^{2n}dx = 2\int_0^a x^{2n}dx,\quad \int_{-a}^a x^{2n-1} dx =0\: \text{（\(n\)は整数）}\) を利用すると， \begin{align} &\int_{-x}^x (ax^3+bx^2+cx+d)dx \\ =&2\int_0^x (bx^2+d)dx\\ =&2\left[\frac{1}{3}bx^3+dx\right]_0^x\\ =&2\left( \frac{1}{3}bx^3+dx \right)\\ =&\frac{2}{3}bx^3+2dx \end{align}
